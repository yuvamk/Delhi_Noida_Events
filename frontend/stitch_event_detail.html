About This Event

Experience the dark heart of the city at DE·NE. We are bringing together the most influential underground techno artists from the NCR region for a one-night-only showcase of industrial soundscapes and immersive visual art.

This isn't just a party; it's a curated editorial experience. Featuring 4D spatial audio and a 360-degree LED installation designed by the 'Neon Collective', we are redefining what nightlife means in the capital.
